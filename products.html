Emotional Companion Robot

The Emotional Companion Robot is an AI product focused on emotional communication and psychological support, capable of understanding human emotions and providing warm companionship and support. It uses advanced emotion recognition technology to analyze users' emotional states through facial expressions, voice tones, and behavior patterns, making appropriate responses.

This robot is designed to be warm and friendly, with a rounded and cute appearance, equipped with an emotional expression system that can express its 'emotions' through lights, sounds, and movements. It is especially suitable for the elderly and those in need of psychological comfort, providing daily communication, emotional support, memory recall, and other services, reducing feelings of loneliness and enhancing psychological health.

Universe Exploration Simulator

The Universe Exploration Simulator is an immersive science education product that combines virtual reality and artificial intelligence, allowing users to explore the vast universe in a virtual environment and experience unprecedented scientific wonders and future possibilities. It is based on the latest astronomical data and physical models, providing realistic and accurate universe simulations.

Users can enter the simulated universe through VR devices, roam the solar system, visit distant galaxies, observe cosmic phenomena such as black holes and supernovas, and even conduct hypothetical cosmic experiments. The AI guide provides professional scientific explanations, answers questions, and guides exploration. This product is not only an entertainment tool but also a powerful science education platform suitable for science enthusiasts of all ages.
